Bakingo UX Flow & Site Architecture
This document breaks down the user journey and page layouts of Bakingo.com. Use this as a blueprint/spec sheet when redesigning the application to ensure you don't miss any critical e-commerce flows or components.

1. Global Components (Present on all pages)
Top Navigation Bar:
Logo (left).
Location/City Selector (e.g., "Deliver to Bangalore"). Crucial for local bakery logistics.
Search Bar.
User Account / Login icon.
Cart Icon with item count badge.
Mega Menu (Category Navigation):
Categories: Cakes, Birthday, Anniversary, By Flavour, By Type, Desserts, Hampers, etc.
Hovering reveals sub-categories and image-rich dropdowns.
Footer:
SEO links (Cakes to Delhi, Cakes to Mumbai).
Company Info (About Us, Contact, Terms, Privacy).
Social Media links.
Trust Badges (Secure payment, FSSAI certified).
2. The Homepage Flow (Discovery)
The homepage is designed to funnel users into specific buying occasions quickly.

Hero Banner Carousel: Large, dynamic banners for current occasions (e.g., Mother's Day, Valentine's, New Arrivals).
Occasion Quick Links (Circular Icons): Birthday, Anniversary, Love, Congratulations, etc.
Trending Cakes / Best Sellers (Horizontal Scroll): Product cards showing Image, Name, Price, and a quick "Add to Cart" or "View" button.
Shop by Flavour: Grid of flavours (Chocolate, Pineapple, Black Forest).
Shop by Category/Type: Pinata, Bento, Photo Cakes, Eggless.
Customer Reviews/Testimonials: Social proof slider.
SEO Content Block: At the very bottom, paragraphs of text optimized for search engines describing their cake delivery services.
3. Category Page Flow (Browsing & Filtering)
When a user clicks "Birthday Cakes" or searches for something.

Header & Breadcrumbs: Home > Cakes > Birthday Cakes
Filters & Sorting (Sidebar or Top Bar):
Sort by: Popularity, Price (Low to High), New Arrivals.
Filter by: Flavour, Weight (0.5kg, 1kg), Egg/Eggless, Type, Price Range.
Product Grid:
Standard grid (usually 3 or 4 columns on desktop, 2 on mobile).
Product Card anatomy:
High-quality thumbnail image.
Badges (e.g., "Best Seller", "Eggless icon" (green dot in square)).
Product Title.
Price (Current Price + Strikethrough original price if discounted).
Review Star Rating (e.g., 4.8 ⭐ (120)).
4. Product Detail Page (PDP) Flow (Decision & Customization)
This is the most critical page for conversions.

Image Gallery (Left Column):
Main large image.
Thumbnail carousel of alternative angles, sliced views, or packaging.
Product Info & Customization (Right Column):
Title & Rating: Cake Name and average star rating.
Price: Prominent pricing display.
Pincode Checker: Input field to check delivery availability and time slots.
Weight Selector: Pills or radio buttons for 0.5 Kg, 1 Kg, 1.5 Kg, 2 Kg. (Price updates dynamically).
Egg / Eggless Toggle: Radio buttons. (Eggless usually adds a small fee).
Message on Cake: Text input field (e.g., "Happy Birthday John").
Action Buttons: Primary "Buy Now", Secondary "Add to Cart".
Add-ons / Cross-sells (Popup or below ATC):
"Make it special" by adding Candles, Party Poppers, Flowers, or Greeting Cards.
Product Description Tabs:
About the Cake (Description, Ingredients).
Delivery Information (How it's packed, delivery rules).
Care Instructions (Refrigeration guidelines).
Reviews Section: Detailed user reviews with photos.
5. Cart & Checkout Flow (Conversion)
Slide-out Cart or Dedicated Cart Page:
List of items with image, selected weight, egg/eggless status.
Quantity increment/decrement.
Add-ons upsell section.
Apply Coupon/Promo Code input.
Price breakdown (Subtotal, Delivery Fee, Taxes, Total).
Checkout Steps:
Login/OTP: Mobile number verification.
Delivery Address & Map: precise location pinning.
Delivery Slot Selection: Standard, Fixed Time, Midnight delivery (often incurs extra charges).
Payment Gateway: Integration with Razorpay/PayU for Cards, UPI, Netbanking.
Order Confirmation: Success animation, Order ID, Track Order link.
